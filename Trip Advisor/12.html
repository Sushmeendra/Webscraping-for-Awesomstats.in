      Could be glorious

      This is a part of the famous ITC chain affiliated to the SPG. It is named after the great rulers across the Mumbai area. It is very close to the airport but far from South Mumbai offices but about 45 minutes from the BKC new hub.
      It is among several hotels all cheek by jowl near the airport. It has competition form them as well as its own sister hotel ITC Central.
      It does not come off that well. It has more attitude and less service.Their recognition of SPG status was inadequate.
      its rooms are OK but service is lacklustre. The breakfast hall looks good but is too small and staff untrained to handle the larger number of guests with finesse. They proclaim much will be served at the table but unlike other hotels the staff will make you wait and force you to go and get it yourself.
      the front office is also very lackadaisical and seems to think guests will just come each time whatever you may do.well they wont!
      Greta location and style but lacks what is needed...service

      If you wanna experience Luxury and Hospitality ... It's completely worthed

      The first day stay in Mumbai at ITC Maratha has become lifetime memorable because of the care, love, attention, attachment, entertainment and above all the friendliness of the staff members.

      It is very close to the International airport and on that day Holi festival was organized for all guests. Sweet Room was grand with all the amenities for a comfortable experience. Maxim (The Counsellor) very patiently explained us about nearby visiting places, events organized and food crawls. His knowledge of the city is very good and deep-rooted. Jasleen from Human Relations was the first to approach us during Holi celebrations. She made sure we feel like a part and enjoy to the fullest. Trupti also from Human Relations attended us very well, always with a broad smile and especially came till outside to see-off. The breakfast at “Peshwa Pavillion – The Center Hall” was too delicious and completely on International standards.

      The approach of the staff members is very open-minded and they made best efforts to make us feel special and comfortable. In short, there was always someone from the staff to chit-chat, entertain and make the stay a pleasant one. ITC fulfilled its aim of luxurious experience and amazing hospitality. I would highly recommend the stay. Looking forward to another one shortly.

      Blissful Experience and Amazing Hospitality!

      I stayed at ITC Maratha for 2 weeks. It is a beautiful hotel with traditionally designed architecture revolving around the marathi culture. I must say their hospitality is worth the appreciation. I was warmly welcomed by Jasleen kaur at the hotel reception and was courteously escorted to the room post all the assistance provided at check-in by her. Being an SPG Platinum member I was given a nice pool view room & it was a very comfortable one. Post working hours me and my colleague enjoyed luxury hours at the upper crust from where you get a beautiful view of the hotel. Throughout the stay Jasleen from guest relations was in contact with us to take stay feedback and to ensure if everything is in place for us - be it at the breakfast or upper crust or anytime in lobby. I remember once I had informed her about some issue with the mini bar which was immediately taken care of before I returned from the office. Much appreciated! Not only this I was surprised to see such a fond farewell done for me and my colleague at the breakfast with a cake which had a message saying "we will miss you" and a bouquet of Red Roses. That was the sweetest gesture. It's been few days & see, I'm back here with my new colleagues. Also, while I was away, Jasleen served as one point contact for any query regarding sales, room query etc. & I've got the similar room again, as requested. Highly recommend this hotel to all the business travellers as well as people looking for a luxury vacation with their family or loved ones !

      Relax, it's just another home in Mumbai

      Well, my stay experience At ITC Maratha is worth a mention because it was legendary. You must go through this review. Firstly it is just 5 minutes away from the International Airport. Secondly it's a perfect place to experience the Indian hospitality the warmth and genuine care for the guest. Thirdly the food is finger licking. you just can't stop yourself from getting a reservation done. Next and most important point is the staff, who is always on their toes, energetic, enthusiastic, positive cheerful and caring. The lobby entrance is Grand with namaste logo, that is a signature which welcomes all the guest with the Indian traditional greeting known as 'Namaste'. The breakfast area is beautiful and has height equivalent to 5 floors of the hotel. It is well lit and well ventilated. No wonders you may have to stand in the queue sometimes to get your turn. The rooms are cosy and well maintained. The garden area is absolutely beautiful & you can enjoy your smokes there. Though I don't smoke. Also they have another smoking area which is next to the main entrance. You will be taken well care of right from the time you enter the hotel till the time you leave the hotel by team guest relations. We were lucky enough to get a reservation here and it was taken care of by Ms Jasleen who maintained the hotel relations with her guests, including us, very well. She would be always there, no matter what the issue is. Not that we had any major one, but still. I could sneak out some information each time I interacted with her.. especially about the fact that it's a "responsible luxury hotel" & have taken several initiatives to ensure their responsibility towards environment. It's something that people are least bothered for these days.
      The icing on the cake was the farewell which surprised me where they came walking to my table at the breakfast with a beautiful cake, yellow roses bouquet and a Maharashtrian special stole. It was lovely. I'd always remember this memorable experience & I'm looking forward to come again soon! Cheers guys! I would recommend this hotel, especially for business travelers & others.
